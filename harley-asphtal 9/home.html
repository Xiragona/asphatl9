ASPHATL

REJENDS

Experience the most authentic motorsport racing game on your PC – where precision meets passion. Lifelike physics, global competition and thrilling victories await. Start your virtual racing career now!

Story Mode

Story Mode is where the original characters from the comic appear as your rivals in the story. Your mission in story mode is to race against these characters and defeat them. In addition, one of the features of this game is "Tuning (Step-Up)." Being able to tune your car (step-up) is a feature of Story Mode.

CUSTOMIZE

Make your car truly yours with extensive customization options. Upgrade your vehicle's performance with new engines, tires, and nitro boosts. Personalize its appearance with a wide array of skins, paint jobs, and decals. Create a ride that not only performs well but looks stunning on the track.

CAMPAIGN

Embark on a thrilling single-player campaign where you'll face diverse missions and challenges. Rise through the ranks, unlock new cars, and conquer increasingly difficult races. Each victory brings you one step closer to becoming a racing legend.
